About Rita

Rita Hurley is a versatile photographer with years of experience working alongside established photography studios, capturing weddings, engagements, maternity sessions, families, and beloved pets. Her background also includes serving as an in‑house corporate photographer and videographer for a major real estate development firm, where she refined her technical skills and storytelling approach. In addition to traditional portrait and event work, Rita offers a deeply meaningful specialty: creating photo and video legacies for individuals nearing end‑of‑life. Through compassionate, documentary‑style sessions, she helps families preserve their loved one’s stories, personality, and presence for generations to come.
